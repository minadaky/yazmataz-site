Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to yazmataz ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when you contact us directly, including:

  • Identity Data: includes first name, last name, company name.
  • Contact Data: includes email address, telephone number.

We do not automatically collect any:

  • Technical Data: such as IP address, browser information, or device information.
  • Usage Data: such as browsing patterns or interaction with website features.

3. How We Collect Your Data

We only collect data through:

  • Direct interactions: When you voluntarily contact us via email or phone.

We do not use:

  • Automated technologies or tracking: We do not use analytics tools, tracking pixels, or other technologies that automatically collect data about your browsing behavior.
  • Cookies for tracking: Our website does not set or use cookies for tracking or marketing purposes.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide services
  • To manage our relationship with you
  • To improve our website, products, and services
  • To send you relevant information about our services (only with your consent)

5. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. We limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, contractors, and other third parties who have a business need to know.

6. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

7. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Request transfer of your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies for tracking or analytics purposes. We do not collect or store any personal data from your browsing activity. We do not use any external services, third-party CDNs, or resources that would set cookies or track user behavior. All fonts and resources are hosted locally on our server.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
